About Us

Know more about theProduction

Cousin Production is a creative duo powered by family, passion, and storytelling. Founded by two cousins who share a deep bond and a love for capturing life's most meaningful moments, we specialize in photography and videography that’s personal, emotional, and cinematic. Whether it’s a wedding, a brand shoot, or a candid life event, we bring two perspectives, one vision, and a whole lot of heart to every frame. More than just a business—this is a family story behind the lens.

  • Behind The story Of & Portrait

    Every portrait tells more than a face — it whispers a chapter of a life, a feeling frozen in time. In this frame, light and shadow dance to reveal not just how someone looks, but who they are in the quiet moments in between.

  • Behind the wedding & photos

    We believe a wedding is not just an event — it’s a living story of love, family, and promises. Through our lenses, we capture the stolen glances, the laughter that echoes, the happy tears, and the unspoken moments in between. Every frame we create is meant to be felt, not just seen — a timeless keepsake that lets you relive your day exactly as it was, long after the last dance.

  • Behind The & Post Production

    The magic doesn’t end when the camera stops rolling — that’s where post-production brings your memories to life. From carefully selecting the perfect frames to crafting cinematic edits, we enhance every detail while preserving the authenticity of your moments. Through color grading, music, and storytelling flow, we transform raw footage into a seamless, emotional experience that feels just as real as the day it happened.

  • Behind The & Comercials

    In the world of brands, every image and frame speaks for you. We craft high-impact commercial ads and photoshoots that blend creativity with strategy — capturing visuals that not only look stunning but also connect with your audience. From concept to final edit, we focus on storytelling, brand identity, and attention to detail, ensuring every shot reflects your vision and leaves a lasting impression.
